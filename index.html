The Coffee Shop

A Place To Meet

The laughing lab is a great place to meet up, study, or just chill. We have the most comfy couches in town and even recliners! We strive to create an atmosphere that is welcoming, cozi, and unique. Unaccompained children will however be given a cute puppy and espresso!

Dogs Are Welcome

Of course dogs are welcome to accompany you at the Laughing Lab! When you arrive we will greet your dog with love and treats. Come meet other dogs that love coffee... oh, and people. Remember to clean up after yourself... and your four legged friend.

Fresh Brewed Coffee

We understand that coffee is a neccesity for some people early in the morning. That's why we do our best to be here at 6:00am on-the-dot, ready to help you get your day started. For those of you who enjoy coffee late at night, yes you, we make sure to brew fresh pots throughout the day.

In House Bakery

Lets be real, what is a coffee shop without homemade treats?! Along with our coffee, we gaurantee to have fresh baked goods all day! That is, until they run out. We even have fresh dog treats for you to give your pets. Come check out what our cooks are creating this week! Menu changes weekly based off our team's mood, season, and recommendations from YOU!
